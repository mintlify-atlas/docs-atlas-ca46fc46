# Your empire's strategic council, on demand

Stellaris Companion reads your save, tracks what is changing across time, and gives actionable strategic advice in your empire's voice. It uses Gemini 3 Flash and a Rust parser for fast extraction.

<Note>
**Privacy-first architecture**: Your save files stay on your machine. Local processing by default. Only your question and extracted game context are sent to Gemini using your own API key.
</Note>

## Strategic analysis

Get deep insights tailored to your empire's current situation:

- "Give me a strategic briefing"
- "Who should I be worried about right now?"
- "What is hurting my economy?"
- "Should I push alloys or research this decade?"
- "How strong is my military compared to neighbors?"
- "Which tech path gives me the biggest edge next?"

The companion uses a **precomputed complete briefing** that includes military, economy, diplomacy, leaders, planets, starbases, and technology data. No tool-calling loop means fast responses.

### Dynamic personality

Advisor tone adapts to your empire's ethics, government, and civics. A militaristic dictatorship gets different advice than a pacifist democracy.

### What leaves your machine

- Your question text
- Extracted game context (numbers, empire names, resource values)
- Sent to Gemini using **your own API key**

### What stays local

- All `.sav` files
- Full game state data
- History database (SQLite)
- Chronicle cache

<Note>
**Discord relay scope**: The relay only forwards `/ask` requests and responses between Discord and your local app. It does not store or log game data.
</Note>

## How it works

```text
User Question
     |
     v
Gemini 3 Flash + empire-specific personality
     |
     v
Complete Briefing JSON (precomputed + cached)
     |
     v
Strategic response
```

### Architecture

- **Electron app**: Primary UI for chat, chronicle, history, settings
- **Python backend**: FastAPI server handling save extraction and LLM calls
- **Rust parser**: Fast Clausewitz format parser built on [jomini](https://crates.io/crates/jomini)
- **Discord relay**: Optional Cloudflare Worker for in-game `/ask` commands

## Perspective and spoilers

Currently, the companion runs in **Strategic Perspective**. Most outputs are player-centric (your empire, your economy, your wars, and your known contacts), but some systems use galaxy-wide signals for stronger analysis.

<Warning>
This can occasionally surface information earlier than normal in-game discovery.
</Warning>

A stricter **Immersive Perspective** with fog-of-war-aware filtering is planned as a future exploration area.
